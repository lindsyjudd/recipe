Ingredients section

Ingredients List

  • Summer Squash, cubed
  • Yellow Onion, large diced
  • Baby Belle Mushrooms, whole
  • Eggplant, cubed
  • 1/3 cup Oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon each: Ppaprika, Oregano, Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions section

Cooking Directions

  1. First: Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Second: In a large bowl combine and toss ALL ingredients.
  3. Third: Arrange on two baking sheets in single layer.
  4. Forth: Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Orgnial recipe "Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" by Ree Drummond of The Pioneer Woman
